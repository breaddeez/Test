About

in buisness since 2021


Bread inc is the company behind many famous movies such as "The NFT Heist" , "The NFT Heist 2" , "Dont Leave me behind" and "Sniper"

Our company is based in Almaty Kazakhstan and consists of the founders friends and family. Bread inc is also a non-profit organisation mainly due to the

fact that we are not able to make any money. This company was first created as a joke within smaller movie projects within the credits of which Adam Burrows edited

because he was bored, but after the inclusion of this after even more movies and projects, it began to grow into a much larger coorperation. We plan to grow

Bread inc much further and have further plans for movies such as the upcomming "Dr Jekyll and Mr. Hyde the movie" as well as expand into using more advanced

Visual effects with the chosen program Blender and Davinci Resolve 17.
